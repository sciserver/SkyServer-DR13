PlateX

PlateX contains information about the plates used for spectroscopic observation. The list below contains the items in Plate that are most commonly searched. For a complete list, see the Schema Browser.

PlateID unique ID for each plate
plate Plate ID (used in Get Plates tool)
mjd MJD (mean Julian Date) of the observation (used in Get Plates tool)
ra right ascension of telescope when the plate was observed
dec declination of telescope when the plate was observed
